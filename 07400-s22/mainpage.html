Overview

The goal of this project is to develop a fully homomorphic encryption (FHE) back-end to CirC, which is a compiler infrastructure for various cryptographic backends. For this project, I will be working with Professor Wenting Zheng and PhD student Edward Chen from the Carnegie Mellon University Computer Science Department.
